# Violet Marker

## Character

- Level: Balanced
- Formality: Low
- Vibe: highlighter, white with violet and lime, modern

## Palette roles

- Ground or large group region: `#CFEE30`
- Primary title, milestone, or key node: `#C5A1FF`
- Secondary group or supporting node: `#CFEE30`
- Ordinary content cards: omit `color` unless semantic emphasis is needed
- Connectors: omit `color` and use the theme-adaptive default

## Native Canvas use

- Apply the ground color to semantic groups, not to a decorative full-board background.
- Apply the primary color to the board title and the most important one or two node classes.
- Apply the secondary color to supporting groups or comparison states.
- Keep the same color meaning throughout the board.
- Use a disciplined grid with one clear accent per section. Keep ordinary cards neutral and reserve saturated color for titles, milestones, or decisive nodes.
- Do not add CSS, gradients, opacity, custom borders, custom fonts, corner-radius fields, or shadow fields.
